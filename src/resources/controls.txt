The player's ship can perform 4 actions:

RIGHT ARROW (→): 	Rotate the ship in the clockwise direction.
LEFT ARROW  (←): 	Rotate the ship in the anti-clockwise direction.
SPACE BAR   (_):	Fire a bullet.
UP ARROW    (↑):	Add speed to the current motion.
H Key       (H): 	Hyperspace jumps your ship to safe location.
P Key       (P): 	Pauses the Game, click again to Continue.
